Thank you for visiting my website.

I currently work as an Assistant Professor in Linguistics. I am the Associate Head of the Department of Languages and Literature, University of Nicosia, Cyprus. I serve as the Director of the Phonetic Lab.

I was honored with the Cyprus Research Award - Young Researcher 2023 in the field of Humanities and Social Sciences. In 2024, I was elected Fellow of the Young Academy of Europe.

I use quantitative methods and computational approaches, such as machine learning, to model speech and language acquisition and identify biomarkers for communication-affecting conditions. I aim to optimize learning outcomes in second language acquisition and develop prognostic and diagnostic tools based on speech and language features for communication disorders.
