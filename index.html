Thank you for visiting my website.

I currently work as Assistant Professor of Linguistics at the University of Nicosia, Cyprus. My primary research interests lie in phonetics, bilingualism, and language disorders. More specifically, my work focuses on:

  • Second-language speech perception and production
  • Cross-linguistic and cross-dialectal interactions
  • Early identification of speech and language disorders

My work has appeared in journals such as the Journal of Phonetics, Applied Psycholinguistics, Journal of Speech, Language, and Hearing Research, and Journal of Neurolinguistics.
